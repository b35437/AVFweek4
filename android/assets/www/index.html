    Week 1 Changes

    1. Less obvious perks of using 'live' data resources include logins and payments. How could Twitter be used in these two scenarios?
    With the ability of ‘live’ data resources twitter like a few other social networks like Facebook allow you to access other sites using your credentials from twitter

    2. Visit Programmableweb.com and search for JSON-P data sources. Why would we need JSON-P, specifically?
    JSON-P allows for the user to access data from an external website as in the use with API’s

    3. Based on the JSON-P search results: what data resource(s) do you think might pair well with which types of Native device features?
    A few I could think of would be the GPS, calendar. But the biggest one of the 2 would be the GPS a lot of the apps developed today ask you to turn GPS feature on for marking your location. Not to mention for travels or even pin pointing somewhere of interest you may be looking for.

    4. Please describe any design and functionality intentions you may have for your Demo app components this month.
    I enjoy what I have learned thus far, and for my application I like the whole twitter implementation and not 100% sure as to what other API I am wanting to use, there are so many that would fit perfect into my application.

    5. Briefly describe some past 'lessons learned' regarding workflow as it relates to research, design, development, etc.
    Verification and error checking are two that are lessons learned back when I first started programming, another would be time management as I still learn from that working full time, school full time, and an any other duties that require me to work late.
